First, the concept

What is vibe coding?

You describe what you want, in plain language. The AI writes the code. You read it, test it, and decide if it's right.
It's not "no effort" — it's a different kind of effort.
The catch nobody tells you

It's not magic. And it's not automatic.

The myth Type one prompt → a finished system appears → you're done. The AI is smart, so whatever it hands back must be good.
The reality The AI is fast, not right. Left unchecked it produces "AI slop" — confident, polished, and quietly wrong. Reading it, testing it, and steering it IS the skill.
Prompt without judgment = slop. Prompt with judgment = a real product. Tonight, we practise the judgment.
The mindset that changes everything

You're not a prompter. You're the architect.

A prompter Types "make me an app", takes whatever appears, and hopes it works.
A builder / architect Decides what to build, scopes it, directs the AI, checks every output, and owns the result.
The AI is your builder — you hold the plan. Tonight you practise being the architect, not the person typing wishes.
AI helps with speed.
You still judge the work.

04
Blueprint Lock

How to write a build blueprint

For each of your 3 sections, answer 3 things. Do the same for each feature.
build-blueprint.md
Section [1] — [name]
  Purpose: why does it exist?
  Content: what's on it?
  Action: what can they click or do?

Feature [1] — [name]
  Trigger: what starts it?
  Feedback: what do they see happen?
Stuck? Ask AI: "Using the EXACT 3 sections and 3 features from earlier — not new ones — plus my brief, turn it into a blueprint: for each section give Purpose, Content, Action; for each feature give Trigger and Feedback. Nothing that needs to fire while the app is closed, like reminders or notifications."

Prompt like this, not like that

Weak "make it look better"
Strong "make each provider card show the star rating in gold, right-aligned"
Weak "add a form"
Strong "add a green WhatsApp CTA button that opens a chat with the provider, pre-filled: 'Hi, I found you on JomFix'"
Say the right word

Know the terms. Get what you mean.

CTA
"Call to action" — the one button you most want tapped. "Chat on WhatsApp", "Book now".
Dropdown
A menu that opens when you tap it, to pick one option.
Carousel
A row of cards you swipe or scroll sideways.
Chevron
The small › arrow that hints "there's more this way".
Modal
A pop-up dialog that opens over the page.
Hero
The big headline section at the top of a page.
The right word = the AI builds the right thing. Vague words get vague apps.
