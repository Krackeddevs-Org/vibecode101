01
Idea Lock

Write your idea in one line

[What it is] for [who it's for].
example

Stuck? Ask AI: "Here's my rough idea: [your words]. Help me turn it into one sentence."
⏱ 5 min
02
Scope Guillotine

3 sections. 3 features. That's it.

Cut everything else — tonight's win is a working site, not a perfect one.

Sections

  • Today
  • Add Habit
  • Streaks

Features

  • Check off a habit
  • Add a new habit
  • See streak count
Rule of thumb: if a feature needs to work while the app is closed (reminders, notifications, alerts) — cut it. V0 builds what's on screen; it can't run in the background.
Stuck? Ask AI: "My idea is [your one-liner]. Suggest exactly 3 sections and 3 features — nothing more. Nothing that needs to work while the app is closed, like notifications — everything happens live, on screen, right now."
⏱ 10 min
03
Brief Lock

How to write a project brief

project-brief.md
Name: what do you call it?
Audience: who is this for?
Goal: the ONE thing it should do
Tone: how should it feel — playful? calm? bold?
Stuck? Ask AI: "Using my idea and the 3 sections + 3 features I just gave you, write me a short project brief — Name, Audience, Goal, Tone — one line each, consistent with that scope."
⏱ 10 min
Station 03 · Worked example

Example: Streaks

project-brief.md
Name: Streaks
Audience: Me — and anyone building a new habit
Goal: Check off habits, watch the streak grow
Tone: Playful, colourful, motivating
04
Blueprint Lock

How to write a build blueprint

For each of your 3 sections, answer 3 things. Do the same for each feature.
build-blueprint.md
Section [1] — [name]
  Purpose: why does it exist?
  Content: what's on it?
  Action: what can they click or do?

Feature [1] — [name]
  Trigger: what starts it?
  Feedback: what do they see happen?
Stuck? Ask AI: "Using the EXACT 3 sections and 3 features from earlier — not new ones — plus my brief, turn it into a blueprint: for each section give Purpose, Content, Action; for each feature give Trigger and Feedback. Nothing that needs to fire while the app is closed, like reminders or notifications."
⏱ 10 min
Station 04 · Worked example

Example: Streaks

build-blueprint.md
Section 1 — Today
  Purpose: see today's habits at a glance
  Content: a list of habits, each with a checkbox
  Action: tap the checkbox to mark it done

Section 2 — Add Habit
  Purpose: create a new habit to track
  Content: a name field and an emoji picker
  Action: tap "Add" to save it

Section 3 — Streaks
  Purpose: see progress build up over time
  Content: each habit's current streak count
  Action: tap a habit to see its history

Style: bright, one bold accent colour, big satisfying checkmarks

Lane Lock

Get into V0

  • 1. Go to your V0 workspace, sign in with Google
  • 2. Start a new project
  • 3. Paste your Blueprint in as your first prompt — not your Brief
Your Brief was for you, to stay focused. Your Blueprint is what V0 actually builds from — fold in the Brief's one-line Tone as a style note.

06
First Build

Build it.

Prompt by prompt, follow your Blueprint. Small steps beat one giant prompt.
Stuck? Raise your hand — a helper comes to you.
⏱ 40 min
While you build

Prompt like this, not like that

Weak "make it look better"
Strong "make the checkmark animate and turn green when I tap it"
Weak "add a form"
Strong "add a form to create a new habit with a name and an emoji picker"
Still stuck?

Ask AI to write the prompt for you

ask Gemini · ChatGPT · Claude
I'm using V0 (a website builder) and I want it to [describe what you want, in your own words].
Help me turn this into one clear instruction I can paste into V0.
Then copy what it gives you, straight into V0.
